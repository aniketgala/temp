Delicious Homemade Pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepper, olives, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, then knead into dough.
  3. Cover and let the dough rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking tray.
  6. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  7. Top with cheese and your favorite toppings.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Enjoy your cheesy homemade pizza!
